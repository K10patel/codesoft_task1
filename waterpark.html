Welcome to our Waterpark

About Our Waterpark

Welcome to our Waterpark. Your Ultimate Destination for Fun and Adventure!

Nestled in the heart of Lonavala, our Waterpark is the premier waterpark for families, friends, and thrill-seekers of all ages. Since our grand opening in 2003, we have been committed to providing an unforgettable experience filled with excitement, relaxation, and memories that will last a lifetime.




Our Water Park Attractions

Kids Play Area

Our Kids' Zone is haven for young adventurers, featuring splash pads, mini slides, and interactive water play structures. Designed for endless fun and excitement, it guarantees a delightful experience for children of all ages.

Twister

Spin with friends and family in a giant cup as you glide across water, creating thrilling moments of laughter and excitement. Perfect for all ages seeking a whirlwind adventure in the heart of our park.

Lazy River

Experience tranquility at our Lazy River, a serene, winding watercourse surrounded by lush landscapes. Drift leisurely on a gentle current, soaking in the sun and scenic views, perfect for relaxation and family enjoyment

Wave Pool

Experience the thrill of the ocean at our Wave Pool! Enjoy the excitement of rolling waves in a safe, controlled environment perfect for swimmers of all ages. Relax on the shore or dive into the action – fun for everyone!

Rain Dance

Experience the ultimate Rain Dance where vibrant music and colorful lights create a festive atmosphere. Dance under cascading showers and enjoy a refreshing, exhilarating party that's perfect for bringing joy and laughter to everyone.

Multi Lane Slide

Race your friends down vibrant, twisting lanes on specially designed mats. Feel the excitement build as you speed towards the splashdown finish. Perfect for a fun, competitive waterpark adventure!

Swirl Whirl

Experience the thrill of our Swirl Whirl! Seated in a 4-person tube, plunge down a high-speed slide into a massive, swirling cone. Feel the rush as you oscillate up the cone’s walls before splashing into the pool below.

Crazy Fall

Plummet at staggering speeds and experience adrenaline within Crazy Fall at our waterpark, a heart-pounding capsule slide that opens into refreshing waters and leaves you craving for more.

Slip N Fly

Experience the thrill of our Ramp Slide at our waterpark. Plunge down a steep slope, speeding through slide before launching into a refreshing pool below. Perfect for thrill-seekers craving excitement and adrenaline.
